# Furaha Talents

Welcome to the **Furaha Talents** branch. This program is dedicated to identifying, cultivating, and showcasing local creative skills and leadership abilities.

## Our Core Programs
* **Talanta Gala:** Our signature community showcase events.
* **Talanta Fellows:** Mentorship pathways for emerging young leaders.

This particular programme hosts the annual **TALANTA GALA**. This is a showcase popularly known as a *Talent Show* where children are given the opportunity to perform and showcase their various talents.

## The Selection Process
There are judges who pick the top 5 children to be awarded and to join the **TALANTA FELLOWS** programme, where they receive guidance by experts in their field on how to nurture their talents and how to make them lucrative.

## School Partnerships
The Talanta Fellows programme is partnered with local schools to ensure that the children have a regular and a constant source of support.
