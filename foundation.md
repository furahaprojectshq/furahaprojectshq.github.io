# Furaha Foundation

The Furaha Foundation is dedicated to helping mothers and single-parent families who shoulder the responsibility of taking care of their children. 

## Economic Empowerment
This program aims to provide viable working opportunities for parents through direct employment within Furaha Projects, allowing them to earn a sustainable income as they provide for their families.

## Health & Female Education
We are committed to providing essential sanitary products and health education for females in our community. Our goal is to ensure that young women understand how to take care of themselves, remain hygienic, manage health symptoms, and understand their bodies to ultimately improve their overall quality of life.

## Healthcare Partnerships
Furaha Foundation will actively partner with local hospitals to get healthcare proffesionals to educate the women, and manufacturing companies that provide sanitary products to effectively aid and expand the support resources available to females in our communities.
