# Furaha Foundation

The Furaha Foundation is dedicated to helping mothers and single-parent families who have the responsibility of taking care of their children. 

## Economic Empowerment
This program aims to provide working opportunities for mothers through employment working directly for Furaha Projects, allowing them to earn an income as they provide for their families.

## Health & Education
We aim to provide sanitary products and reproductive health education to bridge resource gaps in our local communities.
